Denver Studio Shirts

Logo Design

The Deloitte Digital Denver Studio was having an identity slump, so I took it upon myself to design some fun logos and printed them on shirts and hoodies for the entire staff in Denver. It was a huge success and defintely gave the studio team a boost in morale. The shirts quickly became the envy of the other studios and others started making new shirts in Seattle and LA, but all the studios still wanted the Denver shirts. I had a lot of fun freshening up my illustration skills, typography skills and shirt design skills.
